1/2  cup  flour
2    tsp  baking powder
1    tsp  sugar
1/3  tsp  salt
1/4  tsp  cream of tartar
4    tsp  butter
2    tsp  shortenting
3    tsp  milk

preheat 450
mix dry
cut in shortening and butter with 
should look like course crumbs
make a well and add milk all at once
mix very, very gently
turn onto silicone mat and form gently into dough
roll and cut or form into shape by hand
bake 11-15 minute
